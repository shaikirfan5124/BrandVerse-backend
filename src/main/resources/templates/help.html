❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is BrandVerse? BrandVerse is a platform that helps users discover popular brands and visit their official websites from one place.
  • Why do some brand websites not open inside BrandVerse? Some websites don't allow embedding for security reasons. Simply click "Visit Official Website" to open them.
  • How do I save a brand? Click the ❤️ icon on a brand card to add it to your Favorites.
  • Is BrandVerse free? Yes, BrandVerse is completely free to use.
